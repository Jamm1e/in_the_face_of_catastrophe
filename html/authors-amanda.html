Amanda Gwen Goldman

Amanda Gwen Goldman, younger sister of Jenn and daughter of Caryn, has a passion for mixing colors and integrating textural elements into her drawings. The book illustrations are complex and gritty and speak to the dialectic between the beauty and lightness of her color presentation as contrasted with the rugged texture reflecting the heaviness under which the illustrations were conceived.

Amanda also loves the artistry of fashion and is dedicated to finding justice and would ultimately like to help falsely incarcerated prisoners.

Hummingbird
The name Amanda Goldman, Jenn’s sister, gave to Jenn for her ability to defy expectations and chart her own path. A hummingbird can fly backwards.
Dogwood
The tree outside her house that provided her with ongoing strength and inspiration.
Rose
Jenn’s favorite flower, her middle name, her company name, and a main ingredient in many of her teas and oils. Like Jenn, the rose is fragrant, divine, pure, and quintessentially feminine
Jennbug
Jenn’s nickname when she was young, written in Sanskrit and tattooed on the back of Amanda Goldman’s leg.
