JENNIFER ROSE GOLDMAN

Jennifer Rose Goldman

Before her stroke, Jennifer Rose Goldman, a 2013 graduate of Skidmore College with a joint degree in religion and philosophy, founded and served as CEO of Essential Rose, an aromatherapy beauty brand that she started in her college dormitory room. Essential Rose packaged and sold teas and skin-care products for inner, rather than outer, beauty. A motivational speaker on wellness and self-care, she created her own content and taught a six-week course called “Lead Your Life.” Jenn is passionate about all things spiritual and loves discovering any new modalities she can immerse herself in.

I am a wellness entrepreneur by trade and mental health advocate and motivational speaker by heart, whose life-changing journey to wellbeing informs everything I do. Depression darkened every corner of my youth until a wilderness intervention at age 16 pulled me from my bed in the middle of the night and dropped me to live in the woods in the Blue Ridge Mountains. This sparked my journey to self-discovery and holistic healing, where I learned about survival, self-care, and leadership development
