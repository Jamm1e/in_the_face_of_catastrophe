PRAISE FOR THE BOOK

WHAT READERS ARE SAYING
A beautiful, exhilarating account of gratitude and resilience, and how one family faced catastrophe and found unexpected blessings. A story of stoicism through unimaginable crisis. Anyone wondering how they’ll overcome even the darkest challenge will find light in these fast-turning pages.
STEVE ISRAEL

Former US Congressman, author, political commentator, and owner of Theodore's Books

It’s easy to lose hope when a friend or loved one struggles with a serious medical condition or becomes critically ill. Jennifer Goldman and Caryn Hirshleifer, in their book, In the Face of Catastrophe, tell us how they remained positive when faced with a devastating loss. Their heartwarming story encourages us all to keep fighting to overcome the odds and provides hope to all who need it. Miracles can happen. And they do. I recommend this book to my constituents and friends who are facing difficult times.
PAUL FEINER

Greenburgh, NY, Town Supervisor

This is an inspiring story of recovery and renewal against the odds. It teaches us how the power of family and community can carry us through life’s toughest times and greatest challenges.
LL COOL J

Rock & Roll Hall of Fame inductee, two-time GRAMMY® Award-winning hip-hop icon, actor, and author

Terrifying and uplifting by turns, In the Face of Catastrophe is a memoir about the everyday suffering and silver linings discovered while working through a health crisis with family members to assist. This idea of presence as a gift is the book’s most tangible and inspiring quality.
FOREWORD CLARION

Reader Review

In the Face of Catastrophe alternates between understandable despair and hopeful bromides, lessons, and insights from the experience, especially on embracing the power of presence in the everyday. Goldman’s journey toward recovery is heartening, and the family’s accounts will offer comfort to others facing similar trials.
